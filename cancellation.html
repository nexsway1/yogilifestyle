Cancellation Policy

Welcome to the School of Yogic Lifestyle's Cancellation Policy. This policy outlines the process for cancelling your enrollment in our courses and our refund policy.

1. Cancellation Process

You may cancel your enrollment in our courses at any time by contacting our support team at schoolofyogiclifestyles@gmail.com.

2. Refund Policy

If you cancel your enrollment within 7 days of purchase, you may be eligible for a full refund. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.

No refunds will be provided after the initial 7-day period.

3. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Cancellation Policy, please contact us at schoolofyogiclifestyles@gmail.com.
